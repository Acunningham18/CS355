The Sunrise

As the universe hits the snooze button on the night, the sunrise emerges like a cosmic chef flipping the switch on a celestial pancake griddle. The sky transforms into a canvas, painted with hues borrowed from the dreams of slumbering stars. It's a grand unveiling, as if nature is pulling back the curtains on a breathtaking performance, and the sun takes center stage, casting its golden spotlight across the waking world.
